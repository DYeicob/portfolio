About Me

Hi! I'm Jacobo Montero Naranjo, a programming enthusiast and an active student at ESEI. I enjoy tackling complex problems and learning technologies that allow me to grow both personally and professionally.

My current focus is built on three pillars: web development, cybersecurity, and neural networks.

In recent months, I have delved deeply into the field of cybersecurity through a course taught by ASOC and a hardware hacking workshop. These experiences have reinforced my interest in understanding how systems work at a low level and how to protect them.

I am looking for opportunities that allow me to apply my knowledge, contribute to real-world solutions with a strong technical and business understanding, and continue developing as a software programmer.
